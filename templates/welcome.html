Money Tales

Welcome to Module 1

This is a finance game where you will play a card game. You will be given a deck and asked to select some sectors. After sector selection, you will be given some companies to select from. Then based on the company and sectors, you will play a situational game where each situation will be drawn randomly from cards in deck three.

What You Will Learn

  • Understanding of different sectors in finance
  • Knowledge about different types of companies
  • How to make strategic decisions based on financial situations
  • How to maximize the success of your chosen companies
